    Double Layer Notation

    In the image shown below, the white is facing up while red is facing front

    Rw/r

    Turn the right layer with M layer up

    Rw'/r'

    Turn the right layer with the M layer down

    Lw/l

    Turn the left layer with the M layer down

    Lw'/l'

    Turn the left layer with the M layer up

    Uw/u

    Turn the top layer with the E layer left

    Uw'/u'

    Turn the top layer with the E layer right

    Dw/d

    Turn the bottom layer with the E layer right

    Dw'/d'

    Turn the bottom layer with the E layer left

    Fw/f

    Turn the front layer with the S layer clockwise

    Fw'/f'

    Turn the front layer with the S layer counter clockwise

    Bw/b

    Turn the back layer with the S layer clockwise

    Bw'/b'

    Turn the back layer with the S layer counter clockwise
